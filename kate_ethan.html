Directions   

A 1915 topographical map of the upper East River Valley.

Gothic, Colorado is a historic mining village and the current site of the Rocky Mountain Biological Laboratory. It is located at 9,500' above sea level, 8 miles north of Crested Butte, Colorado.

Ethan spent a winter at RMBL as a caretaker in a snowbound cabin 4 miles from where they stop plowing the road in the winter. Kate would drive up from Denver most weekends, skiing out at 4AM Monday morning in order to make an early staff meeting at her stressful journalism job. If that's not an overly on-the-nose metaphor for the importance of commitment and the challenges and rewards of marriage, we're not sure what is.

Getting here requires a little work. If you are willing to pay more, the airport in Gunnison, Colorado will get you 45 minutes away, the airport in Montrose will get you 2 hours away, and the airport in Grand Junction will get you 2.5 hours away. Otherwise, Gothic is roughly a 5 hour drive from Denver Internation Aiport, and roughly 7 hours from Salt Lake International Airport. For Google Maps directions, just click here.

Food & Drink   

A typical refreshment scenario on Gothic Mountain.

One of today's hottest wedding trends involves preparing and serving food for assembled guests. For this year's event, we'll be hopping on the bandwagon, with both a food truck and bartending on the evening of the 2nd, and a low-key brunch on the morning of the 3rd. We will have gluten free and vegetarian options available. If you are hard-line vegan or something, drop us a line ahead of time and we'll make sure you don't go hungry.
